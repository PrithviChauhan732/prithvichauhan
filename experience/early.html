IIM Bangalore, water-access research

Dec 2023 - Feb 2024 · Research intern → research assistant · Prof. Aditya Shrinivas

Drinking-water access in India, 1991-2022, three decades of survey data, one question: who actually gets water?

  • Organized and analysed 15 national surveys (NFHS, NSSO, IHDS) spanning 1992-2020, finding a 17% national increase in tap-water access, with 64% of rural households still without it in 2020.
  • Exposed the gender disparity in water fetching (75% done by women), with male involvement rising from 10.5% to 21% over the period.
  • Ran heterogeneity analysis across states, castes, and income groups; found government scheme impact was negligible (<1% receiving help) and identified states for causal policy analysis using IMIS data.
